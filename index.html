WhoPay

Collaborative bill splitting web app with OCR receipt scanning

This is a project my team created during Hack&Roll 2016, a 24-hour hackathon held by NUS

WhoPay is an web application to facilitate bill splitting for group outings or fund pooling. WhoPay makes use of OCR technology and incorporating a collaborative aspect to quicken the process of bill splitting. Light weight, minimalist and mobile-friendly WhoPay is easy and intuitive to use.

Try it out at https://whopay.herokuapp.com

Sheares Intranet

Intranet system to manage NUS Sheares Hall resident's CCA and points allocation

Intranet system allows the admin to create, edit and delete resident accounts, CCAs, allocate CCA memberships to the residents and assign points based on their performance. Other features include import, export and automatic archiving of data.
