Democracy
 
 
 
The Democracy mode chooses the most popular input provided during a 20 second voting period.
It allowed the followers to overcome challenges thought to be impossible in Anarchy mode.
The ways of Democracy are worshipped by the followers of the Dome Fossil, being the respective counterpart of Anarchy and the Helix Fossil
Anarchist scorn upon Democracy as they believe it cheapens the experience, rendering the journey meaningless
